About Us

"Step into the world of enchantment and wonder at Natsukashii Kafe. Here is a magical place where dreams come true and smiles are served with every cup of joy. Insipired by the meaning of the Japanese word "Natsukashii", (adj.) happy recollection of an event or memory in the past; we want you to feel grateful to have lived the memory, and moving forward in life. At Natsukashii Kafe, we believe in the power of joy that a cafe experience can bring. So come on in, embrace the magic, and let your happy memories soar at Natsukashii Kafe. Also, the gentle aroma of freshly brewed matcha, the soft glow of lanterns, and the soothing melodies of Japanese music create an ambience that encourages relaxation and contemplation. Whether you are seeking a quite moment alone or engage in lively conversations with friends, we can't wait to share the moments of joy, laughter, and wonder with you. Remember, in this little corner of the world, happiness is brewed with love!"

Our Vision

"To be a cozy spot where warmth, joy, and kindness embrace every guest, creating a place of comfort and connection that feels like home."

Our Mission

"To create a place where you can escape the ordinary and indulge in extraordinary moments. We are dedicated to offering an unforgettable dining experience that encapsulates the beauty, precision, and harmony that is deeply rooted in Japanese culinary traditions. As well, we pride ourselves on brewing happiness in every cup we serve."
